# Naming

## Chosen Name: Helios

`Helios` emphasizes verified flows: workflows that are executable, evidence-backed, and auditable. It avoids sounding like a direct port of another platform and fits the enterprise promise better than a purely generative name.

## Product Line

- `Helios`: The generic Go-native AI Workflow Compiler and runtime.
- `SKUFlow`: The MINISO-style new product development scenario shell powered by Helios.

## One-Line Pitch

Helios turns a business goal into an executable, role-scoped, evidence-backed workflow that teams can run, audit, and reuse.
